About Me

I am currently a doctoral graduate student at Washington University in St. Louis,


in their Human and Statistical Genetics program, and I have a B.S. in Molecular Biology


and Bioinformatics from BYU in Utah. In an effort to expand my skillset, be productive


in my spare time, and expand my knowledge into other fields, I have started developing this portfolio.



This site is dedicated to showcasing how the data science skills I have developed,


both in my formal education and my spare time, can be applied to topics that aren't


necessarily related to my field of study. Here, you will find a collection of data-driven


stories, as well as some discussion of the tools I used and my thoughts on the


advantages and disadvantages of the my methods and datasets.
